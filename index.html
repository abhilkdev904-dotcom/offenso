THE TILIA EXPERIENCE

Our space is designed for lingering conversations.

At Tilia, we wanted to build a sanctuary away from the hurried pulse of everyday life. Wrapped in natural textures, calming olive palettes, and light linen finishes, our cafe and dining garden is the ideal backdrop to share culinary artistry.

Every single leaf, dish, and cup of artisan coffee is crafted with direct connection to our sustainable ethos. We invite you to sit back, breathe in the fresh sage, and enjoy the visual poetry of slow living.

CHEF'S INTEGRITY

Crafting Muted Harmony On Your Plate

Every ingredient must have purpose. We embrace olive oils, fresh gardens, raw ceramics, and simple culinary expressions.

Cold-Pressed Olives

We harvest our private estate oil directly from the hills of the Mediterranean, bringing a golden, peppery nuance to all custom pasta dough and pan-sears.

Botanical Design

Handcrafted clay dishware, earthy walls, and native olive olive trees bring natural symmetry indoors. A perfect blend of clean Scandinavian style and Italian rustic charm.

Poured with Intention

Our flour is milled fine daily, then kneaded exclusively by hand. We believe high-grade, naturally fermented wheat retains flavor profiles and digestibility.
